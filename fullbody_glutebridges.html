Glute Bridges [15 reps]

Strengthens glutes, lower back, and hamstrings.

How to Perform

Step 1: Lie on your back, knees bent, feet flat on the floor.

Step 2: Lift hips toward the ceiling, squeezing glutes.

Step 3: Hold for 1–2 seconds at top.

Step 4: Lower back down slowly.


Beginner Tips

• Keep core tight to avoid arching lower back.

• Push through heels, not toes.

• Control movement, don’t rush.
