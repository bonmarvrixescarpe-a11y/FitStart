Mountain Climber [16 reps]

A dynamic exercise that works your core, legs, and shoulders.

How to Perform

Step 1: Start in a push-up position with arms straight and core tight.

Step 2: Bring your right knee toward your chest.

Step 3: Switch legs quickly, pulling your left knee in.

Step 4: Continue alternating smoothly.


Beginner Tips

• Move at a comfortable speed.

• Keep your hips low and body straight.

• Don’t let your hands shift or slide forward.
