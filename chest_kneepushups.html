Knee Push-Ups [12 reps]

A great beginner version to learn proper push-up form.

How to Perform

Step 1: Start on your knees with hands slightly wider than shoulders.

Step 2: Keep your body straight from head to knees.

Step 3: Lower your chest toward the ground.

Step 4: Push back up to the starting position.


Beginner Tips

• Cross your feet behind you for balance.

• Keep your hips from lifting too high.

• Lower slowly for better muscle engagement.
