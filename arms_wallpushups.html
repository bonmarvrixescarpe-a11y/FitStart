Wall Push-Ups [12 reps]

A perfect push-up variation for beginners and seniors.

How to Perform

Step 1: Stand facing a wall and place your hands on it at shoulder height.

Step 2: Lean forward by bending your elbows.

Step 3: Push back to the starting position.

Step 4: Keep your body straight throughout.


Beginner Tips

• Stand further away for more difficulty.

• Keep your core tight to prevent arching.

• Move slowly and with control.
