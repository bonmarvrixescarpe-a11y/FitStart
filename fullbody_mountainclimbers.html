Mountain Climbers [16 reps]

A cardio and core strengthening full-body exercise.

How to Perform

Step 1: Start in a plank position.

Step 2: Bring one knee toward your chest.

Step 3: Quickly switch legs, simulating running.

Step 4: Keep hips down and core tight.


Beginner Tips

• Start slowly, then increase speed.

• Focus on form, not speed.

• Keep breathing steadily.
