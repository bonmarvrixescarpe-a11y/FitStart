Knee Push-Ups [14 reps]

A beginner-friendly push-up to build chest and arm strength.

How to Perform

Step 1: Start in a push-up position with knees on the ground.

Step 2: Place hands slightly wider than shoulders.

Step 3: Lower chest toward the floor slowly.

Step 4: Push back up to starting position.


Beginner Tips

• Keep back straight from head to knees.

• Move slowly for better form.

• Keep core tight for stability.
