Alternating Lunges [14 reps]

Build strength and stability in legs and glutes.

How to Perform

Step 1: Stand upright, feet together.

Step 2: Step forward with one leg and bend both knees 90°.

Step 3: Push through the front heel to return to standing.

Step 4: Alternate legs and repeat.


Beginner Tips

• Keep torso upright.

• Don’t let front knee go past toes.

• Control the movement, no bouncing.
