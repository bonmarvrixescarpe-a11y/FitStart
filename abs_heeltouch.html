Heel Touch [20 reps]

An effective oblique-focused exercise that shapes your sides.

How to Perform

Step 1: Lie on your back with knees bent and feet flat on the floor.

Step 2: Lift your shoulders slightly off the ground.

Step 3: Reach your right hand toward your right heel.

Step 4: Switch sides and touch your left heel.


Beginner Tips

• Keep your core tight while moving side to side.

• Don’t strain your neck — keep it relaxed.

• Move slowly to feel the obliques working.
