Side-Lying Leg Lift [12 reps each side]

Strengthen your outer thighs and hips with this simple exercise.

How to Perform

Step 1: Lie on your side with legs stacked straight.

Step 2: Lift the top leg slowly as high as comfortable.

Step 3: Lower it slowly without touching the bottom leg.

Step 4: Repeat on the other side.


Beginner Tips

• Keep your core engaged for stability.

• Move slowly to avoid swinging.

• Bend the top leg slightly if it's too hard.
