Leg Raises [10 reps]

A great exercise that targets your lower abs.

How to Perform

Step 1: Lie flat on the floor with your legs straight.

Step 2: Place your hands under your hips for support.

Step 3: Slowly lift your legs upward until they form a 90° angle.

Step 4: Lower your legs slowly without letting them touch the floor.


Beginner Tips

• Move slowly to feel your lower abs working.

• Keep your back flat to avoid pain.

• Bend your knees if it's too difficult.
