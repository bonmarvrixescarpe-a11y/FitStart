Inchworms [8 reps]

A great stretching and warm-up exercise for the whole body.

How to Perform

Step 1: Stand tall with legs straight.

Step 2: Bend forward and place hands on the floor.

Step 3: Walk your hands forward until in a plank position.

Step 4: Walk your feet forward back to standing.


Beginner Tips

• Keep your legs as straight as possible.

• Move slowly to feel the stretch.

• Don’t rush — control your movements.
