Wall Calf Raises [12 reps]

Strengthen your calves and improve balance.

How to Perform

Step 1: Stand facing a wall, hands lightly on it.

Step 2: Raise your heels as high as possible.

Step 3: Hold briefly, then lower slowly.


Beginner Tips

• Use the wall for balance.

• Avoid bouncing.

• Move slowly for better calf activation.
