Donkey Kicks [16 reps]

Target your glutes and hamstrings with this simple move.

How to Perform

Step 1: Start on all fours, hands under shoulders and knees under hips.

Step 2: Lift one leg up, keeping the knee bent at 90°.

Step 3: Lower slowly and repeat on the other leg.


Beginner Tips

• Keep core tight for stability.

• Avoid arching your back.

• Move slowly to feel glute engagement.
