Box Push-Ups [12 reps]

A very easy push-up variation perfect for complete beginners.

How to Perform

Step 1: Begin on all fours with hips stacked above knees.

Step 2: Place hands under your shoulders.

Step 3: Lower your chest toward the ground.

Step 4: Push back up.


Beginner Tips

• This is the easiest push-up variation — great for learning form.

• Keep your weight balanced.

• Don’t round your back.
