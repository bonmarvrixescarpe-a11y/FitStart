Sumo Squat Calf Raises [12 reps]

Work your calves and inner thighs simultaneously.

How to Perform

Step 1: Stand with feet wide, toes slightly outward.

Step 2: Lower into a squat, bending knees and pushing hips back.

Step 3: Raise your heels while in squat position.

Step 4: Lower heels and return to standing slowly.


Beginner Tips

• Keep chest upright and knees aligned with toes.

• Engage core for balance.

• Move slowly to prevent knee strain.
