Diamond Push-Ups [6 reps]

Targets the triceps and inner chest for better arm strength.

How to Perform

Step 1: Get into a push-up position with hands close together forming a diamond shape.

Step 2: Keep your body straight from head to heels.

Step 3: Lower your chest toward your hands.

Step 4: Push back up and repeat.


Beginner Tips

• Keep elbows close to your body.

• If it’s too hard, do it on your knees.

• Maintain a straight back to avoid injury.
