Reclined Rhomboid Squeezes [12 reps]

Improve posture and upper-back strength with this beginner exercise.

How to Perform

Step 1: Lie on your back with arms out to the sides.

Step 2: Squeeze shoulder blades together and lift slightly off the floor.

Step 3: Hold for a few seconds.

Step 4: Lower back down slowly and repeat.


Beginner Tips

• Move slowly to engage muscles effectively.

• Avoid straining your neck.

• Focus on squeezing shoulder blades together.
