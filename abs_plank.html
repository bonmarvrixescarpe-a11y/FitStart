Plank [20 sec]

One of the best exercises for core strength and stability.

How to Perform

Step 1: Get into a push-up position and rest on your forearms.

Step 2: Keep your body straight from head to heels.

Step 3: Tighten your abs and hold the position.

Step 4: Breathe normally and avoid lowering your hips.


Beginner Tips

• Start with 10–20 seconds.

• Don't raise or drop your hips.

• Focus on breathing to stay stable.
