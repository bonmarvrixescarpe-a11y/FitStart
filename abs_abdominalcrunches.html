Crunches [10 reps]

A simple exercise to strengthen your upper abs.

How to Perform

Step 1: Lie on your back with knees bent and feet flat on the floor.

Step 2: Place your hands lightly behind your head.

Step 3: Lift your shoulders off the floor using your abs.

Step 4: Lower slowly and repeat.


Beginner Tips

• Keep your lower back on the floor.

• Don’t pull your neck.

• Move slowly for better results.
