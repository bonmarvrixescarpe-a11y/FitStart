Wide Arm Push-Ups [10 reps]

A chest-focused variation that reduces tricep stress.

How to Perform

Step 1: Start in a push-up position with hands wider than shoulder-width.

Step 2: Lower your chest toward the ground.

Step 3: Push back up.

Step 4: Keep your core tight and body straight.


Beginner Tips

• Don’t flare your elbows too much.

• Maintain smooth movement up and down.

• Good alternative for people with weak triceps.
