Rhomboid Pulls [12 reps]

Strengthen your upper back and improve posture.

How to Perform

Step 1: Hold a resistance band in front of you with arms extended.

Step 2: Pull the band apart by squeezing your shoulder blades together.

Step 3: Slowly return to starting position.

Step 4: Repeat for desired reps.


Beginner Tips

• Keep arms straight but elbows soft.

• Focus on squeezing shoulder blades, not just arms.

• Move slowly for proper muscle engagement.
