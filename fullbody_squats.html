Squats [12 reps]

A foundational lower-body exercise for beginners.

How to Perform

Step 1: Stand with feet shoulder-width apart.

Step 2: Push your hips back and bend knees to lower down.

Step 3: Keep chest upright and back straight.

Step 4: Push through your heels to return to standing.


Beginner Tips

• Don’t let knees go past toes.

• Keep core tight throughout.

• Go slow to perfect form.
