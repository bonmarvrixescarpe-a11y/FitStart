Push-Ups [10 reps]

Classic exercise to strengthen chest, arms, and core.

How to Perform

Step 1: Start in plank position with hands shoulder-width apart.

Step 2: Lower your chest toward the floor.

Step 3: Push back up to start position.

Step 4: Keep body straight throughout.


Beginner Tips

• If difficult, do knee push-ups first.

• Breathe out when pushing up.

• Maintain a tight core.
