Russian Twist [20 reps]

A great beginner exercise that targets your abs and obliques.

How to Perform

Step 1: Sit on the floor with your knees bent and feet slightly raised or resting on the ground.

Step 2: Lean back slightly while keeping your back straight.

Step 3: Twist your torso to the right and touch the floor beside you.

Step 4: Twist to the left and repeat the motion.


Beginner Tips

• Keep your core tight while twisting.

• Move slowly for better results.

• If it's too hard, keep your feet on the ground.
