Push-Ups [10 reps]

A classic upper-body exercise that strengthens chest, arms, and core.

How to Perform

Step 1: Get into a plank position with hands slightly wider than shoulders.

Step 2: Lower your body until your chest nearly touches the ground.

Step 3: Push back up to the starting position.

Step 4: Keep your body straight throughout.


Beginner Tips

• If too difficult, do knee push-ups first.

• Avoid letting your hips sag.

• Look slightly forward, not straight down.
