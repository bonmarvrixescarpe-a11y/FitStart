Hindu Push-Ups [10 reps]

A full-body push-up variation that builds flexibility and strength.

How to Perform

Step 1: Start in a downward dog position (hips raised).

Step 2: Scoop your chest down toward the floor.

Step 3: Lift your chest into an upward-facing dog position.

Step 4: Push your hips back up to the starting position.


Beginner Tips

• Move slowly — this exercise is about control.

• Keep breathing steady.

• Great for shoulder mobility and chest strength.
