Diagonal Plank [10 reps]

A core exercise focusing on balance and stability.

How to Perform

Step 1: Begin in a plank position on your hands.

Step 2: Lift your right arm and left leg at the same time.

Step 3: Hold for a moment while keeping balance.

Step 4: Switch sides and repeat.


Beginner Tips

• Keep your hips steady.

• Lift slowly to avoid losing balance.

• Engage your core throughout the movement.
