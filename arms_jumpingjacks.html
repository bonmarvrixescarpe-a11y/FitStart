Jumping Jacks [20 sec]

A great full-body warm-up exercise that boosts heart rate.

How to Perform

Step 1: Stand tall with feet together and arms at sides.

Step 2: Jump your feet apart while raising your arms overhead.

Step 3: Jump back to the starting position.

Step 4: Repeat at a steady pace.


Beginner Tips

• Land softly to protect your joints.

• Keep your core tight while moving.

• Start slow and increase speed gradually.
