Backward Lunge [14 reps]

Work your legs and glutes with this beginner-friendly exercise.

How to Perform

Step 1: Stand with feet hip-width apart.

Step 2: Step back with one leg, lowering both knees to 90°.

Step 3: Push through the front heel to return to standing.

Step 4: Repeat with the other leg.


Beginner Tips

• Keep torso upright.

• Don’t let the back knee touch the floor.

• Move slowly for better balance.
